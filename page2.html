Our story

We started off as a small group of friends frustrated at a lack of soccer spots in Singapore and together, we managed to create this utopia for aspiring and recreational footballers. Come visit our facilities and enjoy with friends and family alike on a fun filled day of football now!

Our facilities include 1 11-a-side pitch, 3 8-a-side pitches, 3 6-a-side pitches, a convenience store, multiple gazebos capable of holding up to 150 people and more!

Shower and changing facilities are included on premises for those rushing for a date or appointment afterwards.
